## 🎯 **How To Use:**

### **View Schedule for Different Periods:**

1. **Quick View (Next 7/14/30 Days):**
   - Click "Next 7 Days", "Next 14 Days", or "Next 30 Days"
   - Schedule automatically loads for that period

2. **Custom Date Range:**
   - Click "Custom Range" button
   - Calendar picker opens
   - Click start date
   - Click end date
   - Click "Apply Range"
   - Schedule loads for your custom period

### **Filter By Type:**

1. **View All:**
   - Click "All Days" tab
   - See work + leave + days off

2. **View Only Work Days:**
   - Click "Work Days" tab
   - See only scheduled work shifts

3. **View Only Leave:**
   - Click "Paid Leave" tab
   - See only approved leave days

4. **View Only Days Off:**
   - Click "Days Off" tab
   - See weekends and unscheduled days
